Research Interests

3D Vision & Mesh Reconstruction

Hand and body mesh reconstruction from RGB. MANO & SMPL-X parametric models. Forward kinematics, linear blend skinning, and the gap between direct vertex regression and rotation-driven generation.

Medical Computer Vision

3D structure recovery for diagnostic and rehabilitation aids. Anatomically valid outputs as a first-class constraint rather than a post-hoc check.

Generative & Spatial Modeling

Diffusion and flow-based 3D generation (actively learning — score matching, DDPM, flow matching). World modeling and spatial intelligence as frameworks for grounding 3D understanding. Cross-modal fusion of visual tokens and structural priors via cross-attention. Not yet an expert in generative models, but this is the direction I'm building toward from my current 3D reconstruction work.

Korean NLP

Multi-label emotion classification with KoELECTRA / BERT. Semi-supervised pseudo-labeling for class imbalance. Threshold optimization and ensemble methods.

Publications

  1. in prep

    Hand Pose Reconstruction and Kinematic Modeling Using Registered 3D Hand Data

    Alfin W. A. Putra, X. P. Yang
    Targeting BMVC / ACCV 2026. Draft — v19 ablations in progress.

    A hybrid RGB + 2D landmark pipeline that predicts MANO rotations via DINOv2 visual tokens, SemGCN-encoded landmark features, cross-attention fusion, and iterative residual refinement, then generates anatomically valid meshes through deterministic FK/LBS using a Korean-shaped MANO template. Achieves PA-MPJPE 7.73mm and rotation error 14.6° (mean) / 13.6° (median).
